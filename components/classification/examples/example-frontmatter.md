# Example Article

The body of the article follows the frontmatter. The five-axis classification
above tells any agent — human or AI companion — how to engage with this
article.

For example:
- `doctrinal_status: clear` means the system holds these claims as settled; an
  AI companion should present them with confidence, not hedge them.
- `content_layer: bridge` means this article engages external sources for
  convergence purposes; new external content can be routed here.
- `breadth: substantial` means most intended sections are present, with some
  identifiable gaps remaining.
- `depth: developed` means the article engages real complexity beyond
  orientation level.
- `craft: clean` means the article passes the minimum threshold for sentence-
  level precision, compression, coherence, register, sovereignty, and
  transmissibility.
